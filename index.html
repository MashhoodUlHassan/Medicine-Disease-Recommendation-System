About Health Care Center

Health Care Center is an AI-powered disease prediction platform developed to help users understand possible health conditions based on symptoms. Our system delivers predictions in seconds, paired with actionable recommendations.

Built with Machine Learning, Flask, Python and curated healthcare datasets, it provides disease predictions along with medications, precautions, diet plans and workout recommendations.
